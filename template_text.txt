本文以珠江公园、海珠湖、晓港公园、东山湖公园、人民公园、云台花园、荔湾湖公园等13个公园作为调查研究对象。根据调查统计，广州市公园共有彩叶植物137种（包括种以下单位），隶属44科，91属，其中以百合科和桑科为主。彩叶灌木种类最多，有62种，占总数的45%，其次是乔木39种，占28%，草本30种，占22%，藤本2种，占1%，竹类4，占3%。斑色叶类植物最多，有52种，占38%。总结了彩叶植物在广州公园的使用效果、配置方法和种植形式，并针对其存在的问题，提出了相关的建议。

Abstract
In this paper, 13 parks have been
 
selected for the survey，such as the Pearl River Park, Haizhu Lake, Xiaogang Park, Dongshan La
ke Park, Liwan Lake Park, People's Park and so on, for the comprehensive analysis of Color–leafed plants in the park usage. According to the investigation and statistics, there are 137 species (including cultivars) of Color – leafed plants, belonging to 44 families, 91 genera. The main body of colored-leaf plants is Liliaceae , next comes Moraceae . Most of colored-leaf plants are cultivars (62 species), represent 45% of the recorded species. There are 62 kinds Colored-leaf shrubs, followed by 39 kinds of trees, 30 
kinds of herbs. Most of colored-leaf plants are Spotted Foliage (52 species), accounting for 38%. The paper summed up the effect and application mode of the Color–leafed plants planting of the parks in Guangzhou, and for its problems, put forward relevant proposals.
